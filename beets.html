Beets

Beets are herbaceous biennial root.

grown for their edible root and young leaves.

Ingerdients

  • vitamin C
    1. helping to protect cells
    2. maintaining healthy skin
    3. maintaining vessels, bones and cartilage.
  • folate
    1. important in red blood cell formation
    2. for healthy cell growth and function
    3. reduce the risk of birth defects of the brain and spine.
  • iron
    1. body needs for growth and development.
    2. to make hemoglobin
    3. iron to make some hormones.
